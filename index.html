About me

I'm Paulo. I'm Portuguese. I was born in 11 May 1992 in a small city nearby Porto where I spent most of my childhood. After finishing high school I moved to Braga to study Informatics Engineering for 7 years.

During my time in university I had the opportunity to join CeSIUM, an association that is related to the Department of Informatics in the university. In that community had the chance to learn a lot and embrace all that knowledge. Above all organizing SEI (https://seium.org/) and JOIN (https://join.di.uminho.pt/) were the most challenging where every year the group focus on creating a better event with the a superb interaction between students and IT companies. This gave me the possibility to improve my organization, leadership and teamwork skills and make amazing friends.

In the last year of my master's all the network formed gave me opening to join Internacional Projects such as Erasmus+ Projects where I could step out of my confort zone, learn new cultures and discuss European Union matters of concern. Being proactive I'm always seeking a new challenge facing it with all my heart and soul one by one.
